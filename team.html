Our Coach

What is iWanna Coach

iWanna Coach is designed to promote cross-cultural awareness, build stronger communication skills, and empower individuals to better cope with acculturation. iWanna Coach service is not therapy or counseling. It offers an easy, accessible, and safe space to express areas of concern.

How It Works

iWanna Coach is based on the weekly unlimited message and/or scheduled voice/video calls; targeting on but not limited to the following areas: academic pressure, working/jobs, relationship, acculturation, emotional management and confidence/resilience.

Goal

We focus on communication, sharing, compassion, and understanding. The goal is to maximize our individual’s experiences in adjustment to a new chapter of their life with supplementary tools and support.

Our Beliefs

We are not acting as therapists or replacing therapy. We believe the availability of guiding support is essential in certain life situations. This is especially important when facing acclimation to a foreign culture. We provide a platform for people who have previous experience with the process to lend a helping hand to others who may be experiencing challenges and other difficulties. iWanna Coach serve individuals from all over the world who are experiencing acculturation difficulties with interpersonal relationships, emotional, academic and vocational concerns. iWanna Coach does not judge individuals based on skin color, ethnicity, national origin, religion, gender, age, disability, sexuality or sexual orientation.

Who are the participants?

iWanna serves individuals who are experiencing cross-cultural difficulties with interpersonal relationships, academic and vocational concerns while transitioning to a new culture from around the world.

iWanna Coach vs Professional Counseling

iWanna Coach is not therapy or counseling. It offers an easy, accessible, and safe space to express areas of concern. It helps to let things out or to find someone who is genuinely willing to listen and providing feedback. iWanna Coach do not make any diagnoses and we do not provide crisis intervention or emergency services. iWanna Coach is not therapists or licensed professional counselors, but individuals who want to help others think through problems and provide guidance toward challenges. Get in touch today and start taking control of your life with the support from iWanna Coach.

iWanna Cares about Your Privacy

iWanna will respect and protect every participant’s personal information. Participants have the absolute right to the privacy of their service with the exception of extenuating circumstances.
